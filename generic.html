Product Description

SolarSprout is innovative self-watering system designed to make plant care effortless and sustainable. Using solar energy and smart automation, it ensures plants receive the right amount of water at the right time.

It also reduces waste and promoting healthy growth.

Materials and components

1.Water pump: It circulates water from the reservoir to the plants. The advantages is that it is efficient, low- energy consumption, and ensures even water distribution. The benefits is that it keeps plants hydrated without manual watering, reducing the risk of overwatering or underwatering.

2.Water tube: channels water from the pump to the plants. It is flexible, durable and resistant to clogging. Its benefits are that it delivers water precisely to plant roots, ensuring, efficient hydration.

3.Water Reservoir: It stores water supply for the system. The advantages are that it is made from repurposed materials, ecofriendly, and easy to refill.

4.Solar-Powered Smart Timer Plug: It controls water pump, turning it on and off at scheduled times. It is solar-powered, app-controlled, and programmable
